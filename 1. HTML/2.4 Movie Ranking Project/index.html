The Best Movies According to Mark

My top 3 movies od all time.

Meet the Robinsons

Growing up, I've been doing lots of mistakes, this movie
have helped me to understand that it is okay to do mistakes
because through them, you'll learn. Just "keep moving forward",
it doesn't matter how many times you do it. Just keep moving forward.

Inception

The story line + the cinematography was a chef's kiss. I don't want
to spoil everyone, but to those who hasn't watched it yet, you should
definitely check this one out!

Encanto

I'm really a fan of animated movies/series, and this movie is also
one of them. It's fun, and has very relatable characters.
